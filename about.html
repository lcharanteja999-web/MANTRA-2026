About Us

Who We Are

Kingdom Roads is a small team that puts together simple, carefully planned trips to some of India's quieter destinations. We started because we were tired of crowded, generic tour packages and wanted to help travellers find places that still feel personal.

What You Get

  • A short list of handpicked destinations, not hundreds of confusing options
  • A cost calculator so you know the price before you enquire
  • A single page booking form that takes under a minute
  • A photo gallery so you know what to expect before you pack
